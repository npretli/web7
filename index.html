Who We Are

The GIS Pros team includes geospatial experts that span a wide range of expertise and can help you make informed decisions based on solid GIS based evidence. We can help you realise your vision from start to finish. This includes identifying the base data needed to address your questions, developing an efficient and well designed workflow to explore and analyse that data, and summarize the results in a way that everyone can understand.

We have experience working with a variety of stakeholders across the country. The application of GIS is far reaching and transcends traditional divisions in sector type, fields of research, etc. From small, independant companies and NGOs to city officials and government organisations, we have experience with all. For more detailed information on the service we offer and projects we have completed, please refer to our Services page.
